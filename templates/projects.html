MLBioIGE: Genetic Impact of SARS-CoV-2 on Pulmonary Fibrosis

Collaboration with bioinformatics and machine learning techniques to identify genetic factors in COVID-19 patients.

This project integrates machine learning with bioinformatics tools to analyze genetic data, aiming to identify potential factors contributing to pulmonary fibrosis in COVID-19 patients. By combining genomic data with advanced models, we seek to improve treatment strategies.

Research presented at the Arkansas State Capitol, focusing on real-time sports analysis with machine learning.
